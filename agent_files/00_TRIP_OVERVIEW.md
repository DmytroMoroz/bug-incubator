## ⛅ MT. FUJI TIP: Hunt for a Sunny Day!

> The Kawaguchiko day trip (Fuji views + onsen) is **weather-dependent**.
> 
> **Strategy:** Check the forecast each evening. When you see a clear day → book the bus for next morning. Don't lock yourself to a fixed date!
> 
> See [Onsen & Fuji Guide](02_onsen_fuji.md) for details.

## Key Reminders

1. **Mt. Fuji Day Trip:** Flexible date - check weather, book bus night before
2. **Cherry Blossoms:** Your dates catch late sakura in some areas
3. **Golden Week:** April 29+ is holiday season - expect crowds
4. **Okinawa Car:** Rental car essential - get International Driving Permit before trip!
5. **Book Early:** Sakurai Tea Ceremony sells out months ahead
