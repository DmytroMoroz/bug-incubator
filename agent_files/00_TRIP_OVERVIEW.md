## Key Reminders

1. **Cherry Blossoms:** Your dates miss peak sakura (late March). Still great weather!
2. **Golden Week:** April 29+ is holiday season - expect crowds
3. **Okinawa Car:** Rental car is essential - get International Driving Permit before trip!
4. **Book Early:** Ryokan, Sakurai Tea Ceremony sell out months ahead
