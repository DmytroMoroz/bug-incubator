## Why Hiroshima

Hiroshima is forever linked to August 6, 1945, when it became the first city to suffer an atomic bombing. Today, it's a vibrant, forward-looking city dedicated to peace. The Peace Memorial and Museum are essential for understanding this history.

**Miyajima Island** (30 min away) offers a beautiful contrast - a sacred island with the famous "floating" torii gate, wild deer, and stunning shrines.

---

## Top Highlights

1. **Peace Memorial Museum** - Powerful, essential experience
2. **A-Bomb Dome** - Preserved ruin, UNESCO World Heritage
3. **Itsukushima Shrine** (Miyajima) - Floating torii gate
4. **Mount Misen** (Miyajima) - Hiking/ropeway with sea views
5. **Hiroshima-style Okonomiyaki** - Layered savory pancake

## Miyajima Highlights

### Itsukushima Shrine
- **High tide**: Torii gate and shrine appear to float on water
- **Low tide**: Walk out to the torii gate, touch it
- Both are beautiful, different experiences

### Mount Misen
- **Ropeway**: ¥1,800 round trip, saves time
- **Hiking**: Momijidani or Daisho-in trail (~1.5h up)
- **Summit**: 360° views of Seto Inland Sea

### Other Spots
- **Daisho-in Temple**: Atmospheric temple at mountain base
- **Momijidani Park**: Maple trees (especially beautiful in autumn)
- **Shopping street**: Between ferry and shrine

## Hiroshima Food

### Must-Try: Hiroshima-style Okonomiyaki

**What makes it different:** LAYERED, not mixed like Osaka-style
- Layers: batter, cabbage, pork, noodles, egg
- Cooked on a griddle in front of you

**Where to try:**

| Place | Location | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **Okonomi-mura** | 4-story building, 20+ stalls | Tourist-friendly, variety |
| **Nagata-ya** | Near Peace Park | Local favorite |
| **Mitchan** | Multiple locations | Claims to have invented Hiroshima-style |

*Source: Japan-guide.com, local recommendations*

### Miyajima Specialties
- **Oysters**: Grilled, fried, raw - Miyajima is famous for them
- **Momiji Manju**: Maple leaf-shaped cakes (many flavors)
- **Anago** (conger eel): Local specialty, try anago-don (over rice)

## Practical Information

### Getting to Miyajima
1. **JR train** Hiroshima → Miyajimaguchi (30 min)
2. **Ferry** Miyajimaguchi → Miyajima (10 min, ¥180)
3. Total: ~45 minutes, easy

### Miyajima Accommodation
| Type | Price | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| **Ryokan** | €150-300 | Traditional, often includes dinner |
| **Hotel/Guesthouse** | €80-150 | More affordable |

**Highly recommend staying overnight** - the island empties at 6pm when day-trippers leave.

### Luggage
- Use **coin lockers** at Hiroshima Station
- Or send luggage ahead to Okinawa hotel (Takuhaibin)

## Important Notes

### Peace Memorial Visit
- **Allow 2-3 hours** minimum for the museum
- **Emotionally intense** - pace yourself
- **Photography**: Allowed in museum (no flash), be respectful
- **Free audio guide** available

### Miyajima Deer
- Wilder than Nara deer - don't feed them
- They may try to eat paper/maps - keep belongings secure
