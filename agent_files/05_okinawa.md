## Why Okinawa

Okinawa is Japan's tropical paradise - a chain of islands with a distinct culture, cuisine, and history. Once the independent Ryukyu Kingdom, it has its own language, music, and traditions. The main island offers beautiful beaches, world-class diving, unique food, and a laid-back atmosphere that's completely different from mainland Japan.

**Weather in late April:** 24-26°C, warm, great for beaches!

4. **Okinawan food** - Completely different from mainland cuisine

## IMPORTANT: Rental Car

**A rental car is ESSENTIAL in Okinawa.** 

Public transportation is extremely limited outside Naha city. The monorail only serves Naha, and buses are infrequent and slow.

### What You Need
- **International Driving Permit (IDP)** - Get this in your home country BEFORE the trip!
- **Book in advance** - April is expensive (~€60-75/day)
- **Pick up at Naha Airport** - Most convenient

### Rental Tips
- Book through **Nippon Rent-A-Car**, **Times**, **Toyota**, or **OTS**
- Get basic insurance (included in most rentals)
- GPS is usually included (set to English)
- Okinawa drives on the LEFT (same as rest of Japan)

## Okinawa Food Guide

Okinawan cuisine is COMPLETELY DIFFERENT from mainland Japan. Influenced by China, Southeast Asia, and post-war America.

### Must-Try Dishes

| Dish | Description | Where |
|------|-------------|-------|
| **Okinawa Soba** | Wheat noodles in pork broth + stewed pork | Everywhere |
| **Soki Soba** | Soba with tender pork ribs | Local soba shops |
| **Goya Champuru** | Bitter melon stir-fry with tofu, egg, pork | Izakayas |
| **Rafute** | Melt-in-mouth braised pork belly | Nicer restaurants |
| **Taco Rice** | Ground beef, cheese, salsa on rice | Casual spots |
| **Umibudo** | "Sea grapes" - popping seaweed bubbles | Seafood restaurants |
| **Sata Andagi** | Okinawan doughnuts | Markets, street food |

See [Full Food Guide](07_food_guide.md#okinawa) for restaurant recommendations.

## Practical Information

### Driving Tips
- Drive on the LEFT
- Speed limits: 60 km/h on regular roads, 80 km/h on expressway
- Gas stations say "full service" or "self" (self is cheaper)
- Tolls on expressway - pay cash or ETC card

### Okinawa vs Mainland Japan
| Aspect | Difference |
|--------|------------|
| **Pace** | Much slower, more relaxed |
| **Food** | Completely different cuisine |
| **Music** | Distinct Okinawan style with sanshin instrument |
| **Language** | Okinawan (Uchinaaguchi) still spoken by elders |
| **Temples** | Fewer - Ryukyu religion focused on sacred groves |

### What to Buy
- **Ryukyu glass** - Beautiful colored glassware
- **Shisa** - Lion-dog statues (one mouth open, one closed)
- **Beniimo tarts** - Purple sweet potato tarts
- **Awamori** - Okinawan rice spirit
- **Salt** - Okinawa is famous for sea salt
